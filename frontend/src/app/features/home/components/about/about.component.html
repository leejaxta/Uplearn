How a Crazy Idea Became a Global Classroom

2008

The Garage Days

Two teachers + one developer + too much coffee. We believed learning shouldn't be locked behind expensive textbooks or rigid schedules.

2012

First 1,000 Students

Our free Python course went viral. We cried. Then we hired our first tutor — a retired professor who taught in pajamas.

2018

Global

Launched in 12 languages. Students from Nepal to Norway. One even finished a course while hiking Everest Base Camp. True story.

2024

What's Next?

AI tutors that adapt to your mood. VR classrooms on Mars (beta). And yes — we're still drinking too much coffee.
